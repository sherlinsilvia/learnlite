Why Use Audio in Lower Bandwidth Learning?

- **Smaller File Sizes**: Audio uses less data compared to video, making it faster to download.
- **Accessible Anywhere**: Learn on the go by listening while multitasking.
- **Focus on Content**: No distractions, just clear information.

- Optimize files for smaller sizes (use MP3).
- Offer downloadable content and transcripts for offline access.
